Therapy can help!

A typical solution for dealing with depression is therapy. You can use therapy When you are stressed and lost with your feelings, need help navigating your feelings, and need tools to manage them. A link to therapy and counseling can be found under resources.

Antidepressants are an option!

A direct way to deal with depression is by taking antidepressants. You can be prescribed antidepressants by a health care professional. You can contact your health care provider for the prescription.

Eating habits matter!

Dieting is crucial to dealing with depression. A recommended diet has high contents of vegetables, fruit, fish, grains, and low animal fat.

Sleeping habits also matter!

Sleeping habits can effectively treat depression. Make sure to sleep at an appropriate time for a reasonable number of hours. Avoid using technology before going to sleep too!

You can always be kind!

Another way to deal with depression that many would not think of is doing simple acts of kindness. A study conducted at Ohio State University found that doing acts of kindness can increase life satisfaction and reduce depression. A link to that research can be found under resources.
