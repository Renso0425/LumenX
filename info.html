What is depression?

Depression, otherwise known as major depressive disorder, is a serious medical illness that negatively affects how you feel, think, and act.

What are some symptoms of depression?

  • Persistent feelings of discouragement, hopelessness, worthlessness, emptiness, discouragement, and sadness.
  • A loss of interest in activities that you or an individual once found enjoyable.

What can cause depression?

Depression can be caused by various events: losing a job, financial difficulty, social isolation, pressure and sadness from academics, and trauma.
