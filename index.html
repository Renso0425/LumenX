Introduction

Context

Hello everyone, my name is Darren Zheng. When I was in the late stages of middle school, I often found myself alone a lot of times. I wasn’t interested in interacting with my peers. I would also find myself experiencing constant hopelessness, sadness, and occasional suicidal thoughts. I wasn’t sure if it was an actual case of depression. I wasn’t sure if I needed therapy or if what I was feeling was normal. I reached out to a therapist in highschool, but I thought that therapy was such a waste of time. Looking back from now, I am in such a better position yet now I’m wondering in what ways I can help other people to know that their feelings are valid, and to educate them on what depression is.

Goal

Right now, due to the covid pandemic, there is a rise in the number of depression cases. Since majority of depression cases often results in suicide, action must be taken. I created this website in hopes of shedding more light on depression. But first, take a quiz about depression to see how much you actually know so far. And after navigating my website, take the quiz again to see if you learned anything new!
